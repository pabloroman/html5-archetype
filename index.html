Archetype [/ˈɑrkɪtaɪp/]: An universally understood symbol, term, statement, or pattern of behavior, a prototype upon which others are copied, patterned, or emulated.

HTML5 Archetype is a very simple HTML5 framework. Some of its features are: responsiveness, a straightforward fluid column grid and great typography. Read more about it (in Spanish) here.
